What is Trioflix Studio ?

Trioflix Studio is a subscription-based streaming service that allows our members to watch TV shows and movies on an internet-connected device.

Depending on your plan, you can also download TV shows and movies to your iOS, Android, or Windows 10 device and watch without an internet connection.

If you're already a member and would like to learn more about using Trioflix Studio, visit Getting started with Trioflix Studio.

TV Shows & Movies

Trioflix Studio content varies by region and may change over time. You can watch a variety of award-winning Trioflix Studiooriginals, TV shows, movies, documentaries, and more.

The more you watch, the better Trioflix Studio gets at recommending TV shows and movies.

Supported Devices

You can watch Trioflix Studio through any internet-connected device that offers the Trioflix Studioapp, including smart TVs, game consoles, streaming media players, set-top boxes, smartphones, and tablets. You can also watch Trioflix Studioon your computer using an internet browser. You can review the system requirements for web browser compatibility, and check our internet speed recommendations to achieve the best performance.

NOTE:A small percentage of devices may not be supported by all plans.

Need help getting set up? Search our Help Center for the manufacturer of the device you're using.

NOTE:The Trioflix Studioapp may come pre-loaded on certain devices, or you may need to download the Trioflix Studioapp onto your device. Trioflix Studioapp functionality may differ between devices.

Plans and Pricing

Each Trioflix Studio Plan determines the number of devices you can watch Trioflix Studioon at the same time and whether you can watch in High Definition (HD), Full High Definition (FHD), or Ultra High Definition (UHD).

You can change your plan or cancel online at any time.

Get Started

To start watching Trioflix Studio:

  1. Visit Trioflix Studio.com/signup.
  2. Choose a Plan.
  3. Create an account by entering your email address and creating a password.
  4. Enter a payment method.

As a Trioflix Studio member, you are charged once a month on the date you signed up.
